How Your Donation Helps

Every dollar you donate makes a difference in the lives of animals in our care. Your generosity helps us:

  • Provide food, shelter, and medical care for abandoned animals
  • Fund rescue operations and emergency veterinary services
  • Support our adoption program to find forever homes
  • Maintain our facilities and provide quality care
  • Conduct community education and outreach programs

Alley Cats Allies is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
