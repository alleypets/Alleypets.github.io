About Us

Dedicated to the welfare of animals since 2010

Alley Cats Allies Animal Shelter is a non-profit organization dedicated to the rescue, care, and placement of animals in need. Since our founding in 2010, we have rescued over 5,000 animals and found forever homes for 4,500 of them.

Our shelter provides a safe and nurturing environment for animals who have been abandoned, abused, or surrendered. We believe that every animal deserves a loving home and the best possible care.

With a team of dedicated staff and volunteers, we work tirelessly to ensure that each animal receives individual attention, proper medical care, and the opportunity to find a perfect match with a forever family.
